# Project Scope: Digital Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis

## Background / Purpose

This project analyzes digital marketing campaign data from Small to Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) across various industries. The dataset covers campaigns run through multiple marketing platforms including email, influencer, content, search engine, and social media.

**Goals:**
- Formulate effective digital marketing strategies based on channel and campaign performance
- Optimize campaigns by identifying key success factors
- Analyze marketing resource allocation across channels, industries, and company sizes
- Examine performance trends across different industries
- Identify factors that determine campaign success

The findings are expected to support digital marketing managers and analysts in formulating effective marketing strategies.

---

## Business Questions

### 1. Channel Performance
- Which marketing channel has the highest conversion rate?
- Which marketing channel has the highest success rate?
- What is the average ad spend for successful vs unsuccessful campaigns per channel?

### 2. Campaign Success Factors
- Which device has the highest usage and conversion rate?
- How does campaign duration affect the success rate?
- Which target audience segment has the highest success rate?

### 3. Regional Performance
- Which top 3 regions have the highest conversion rate?
- What is the average audience reach per region?
- Does higher audience reach correlate with campaign success?

---

## Scope

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Channel Performance | Analysis of marketing channel effectiveness and ad spend |
| Campaign Success Factors | Key drivers of campaign success including device, duration, and audience |
| Regional Performance | Geographic analysis of campaign conversion and reach |

---

## Out of Scope

- This project does not include predictive modeling or machine learning
- No real-time data analysis
- No time-based trend analysis (no date column in dataset)
- Implementation of marketing recommendations is not included
